# PESTLE Analysis

**Purpose**: Understand macro-environmental factors

## The Six Factors

| Factor | Focus | Examples |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Political** | Government, policy | Regulations, stability, trade policy, taxation |
| **Economic** | Economy, markets | Growth, inflation, interest rates, unemployment |
| **Social** | Society, culture | Demographics, trends, attitudes, lifestyle |
| **Technological** | Technology | Innovation, R&D, automation, digital transformation |
| **Legal** | Laws, compliance | Employment law, IP, health & safety, contracts |
| **Environmental** | Environment | Climate, sustainability, resources, carbon |

## Questions to Ask

- For each factor: How does this affect us now? In 5 years?
- Which factors are most critical to our business?
- What opportunities do these factors create?
- What threats do they pose?
- How are these factors changing?

## Process

1. Identify relevant factors in each category
2. Assess current impact (positive/negative/neutral)
3. Assess future trends (increasing/decreasing/stable)
4. Prioritize by impact and certainty
5. Identify strategic implications
6. Build into strategic planning

## Tips

- Focus on factors relevant to YOUR context
- Consider interactions between factors
- Update regularly (environment changes)
- Use as input to SWOT analysis

## Output

Environmental scan with strategic implications
