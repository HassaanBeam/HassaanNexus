# SCAMPER

**Purpose**: Systematic creativity technique

## The Seven Techniques

| Letter | Technique | Key Question |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| **S** | Substitute | What can we swap out? |
| **C** | Combine | What can we merge together? |
| **A** | Adapt | What can we copy from elsewhere? |
| **M** | Modify/Magnify | What can we change, enlarge, or shrink? |
| **P** | Put to other uses | What else could this be used for? |
| **E** | Eliminate | What can we remove? |
| **R** | Reverse/Rearrange | What if we flip it or reorder? |

## Detailed Questions

### Substitute
- What materials, components, or people could we substitute?
- What process could we use instead?
- What if we changed the rules?

### Combine
- What ideas, features, or products could we combine?
- What if we merged with another organization?

### Adapt
- What else is like this? What could we copy?
- What ideas from other industries could we adapt?

### Modify
- What if we made it bigger? Smaller? Faster? Slower?
- What if we changed the color, shape, or form?

### Put to Other Uses
- What else could this be used for?
- Who else could use this? In what context?

### Eliminate
- What can we remove without losing value?
- What if we simplified it drastically?

### Reverse/Rearrange
- What if we did it in reverse order?
- What if we swapped components?

## Output

List of creative modifications and improvements
